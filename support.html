Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchase?

Open the app, tap "Go Premium" and then tap "Restore previous purchase". Your purchase will be restored automatically if you are using the same Apple ID or Google account used for the original purchase.

How long does access last after purchase?

Access lasts 6 months from the date of purchase. There is no automatic renewal — you will need to purchase again if you wish to continue after the 6-month period.

My questions are not loading. What should I do?

Try closing and reopening the app. If the problem persists, try uninstalling and reinstalling the app. Your purchase will be restored using the "Restore previous purchase" option.

I have a promo code. How do I use it?

Tap "Go Premium" on the home screen, then select the "Redeem Code" tab. Enter your code and tap "Activate Code".

Can I use the app on multiple devices?

Yes. Sign in with the same Apple ID (iOS) or Google account (Android) and use "Restore previous purchase" to activate your access on any device.

Are these apps affiliated with ISACA?

No. These are independent study tools and are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or associated with ISACA in any way. ISACA®, AAIR®, AAISM®, and CRISC® are registered trademarks of ISACA.
